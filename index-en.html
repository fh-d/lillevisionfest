Lille Vision Festival is giving the opportunity to grasp a wide panorama of digital cultures and groundbreaking creation via an ambitious and multidisciplinary line-up.
This major event at the scale of the European Lille Metropole is gathering people together with a single goal: using design and the serendipity of mutual creation to tackle issues of today and tomorrow.

Philosophy

Lille Vision Festival is primarily the story of an encounter. That of music and digital art.
Lille Vision Festival is also a mad desire. That celebrate creativity in all its forms, it is rooted in dance, graphic design, photography, video, music, and design. To create bridges and transcend the usual boundaries.
Finally, it's a double opportunity. That to offer artists an open forum in an exceptional setting. And that offering the public a dip in the wings of a creative process. With a contemporary prism and Made in France for the first edition of a long series to come.
